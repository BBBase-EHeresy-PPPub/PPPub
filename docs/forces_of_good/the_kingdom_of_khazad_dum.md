## ARMY BONUS

*"The wealth of Moria lay not in Gold or Jewels, but in Mithril" - Friendly Dwarf models with either the Khazad-dum or Moria keyword may re-roll To Wound rolls of a 1 when fighting in close combat.*

## SHIELDBEARER - 60 POINTS

**DWARF, KHAZAD-DUM, INFANTRY, HERO - MINOR HERO**

| Mv | F  | S | D | A | W | C |
|:----:|:----:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 5" | 5/4+ | 4 | 8 | 2 | 2 | 6 |

**WARGEAR**

- Dwarf armour, shield, and axe.

**SPECIAL RULES**

- *In Defence of the King*: At the start of the game, nominate a single Dwarf Hero model with the highest Heroic Tier for the Shieldbearer to protect. If the nominated Hero model is Engaged in a Fight within 3" of the Shieldbearer, then the Shieldbearer may call a Heroic Combat without using Might. If the Heroic Combat is successful, they must use the following move to join their nominated Hero model's combat.

- *Lead by Example*: Friendly Dwarf models within 12" of a Shieldbearer may re-roll failed Courage tests.

The Shieldbearer serves to inspire their kin with their stalwart example and is charged with the protection of the king, willing to cut through ranks of their enemies to reach their liege.

This concludes the heroes of the Kingdom of Khazad-dum portion of the army list, including the army bonus that emphasizes the valor and combat prowess of Dwarves, especially in the context of their legendary resilience and fighting skill in close quarters.
