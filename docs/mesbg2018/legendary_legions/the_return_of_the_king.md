## ADDITIONAL RULES

- This army must include both Aragorn and the King of the Dead. Aragorn is always the army's leader. Aragorn, Legolas and Gimli may all lead Warriors of the Dead and Riders of the Dead.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"The King of the Dead"** - The King of the Dead gains the [Harbinger of Evil] special rule.

**"The Heir of Isildur"** - Aragorn does not have to pay any points for Andúril, Flame of the West, he automatically has this upgrade - it is free.

**"Fight for us and Regain Your Honor"** - Spirit models from this army list count as being in range of a [banner][Banner] if Aragorn is within 6".
