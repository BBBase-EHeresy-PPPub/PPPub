## ARMY COMPOSITION

This army may include: Smaug.

## ADDITIONAL RULES

The Desolator of the North army list does not get a special rule - having a massive fire-breathing Dragon is a deadly enough bonus as it is!

- **Breathe Fire** `ACTIVE`

Spend a Will point to Breath Fire, which is like a [bow][Bow] with range 18". If the shot hits, the target and all models within 2" are [Set Ablaze], and suffer a Strength 10 hit. Cavalry models suffer this on both the rider and mount. Fate may prevent these wounds, but any model that suffers just a single wound is slain.

- ***Desolator of the North** `PASSIVE`

Smaug may spell a free Will point each turn. Also re-rolls To Wound rolls of 1 when making Strikes against Dwarf models.

- **Immovable Object** `PASSIVE`

Cannot be Knocked to the Ground, Back Away, Trapped or move against his will. His opponent always Backs Away in a Fight.

- **Missing Scale** `PASSIVE`

If a model rolls a natural 6 on a To Hit roll against Smaug, and the subsequent To Wound roll is successful, he will suffer a number of Wounds equal to the strength of the missile weapon.

- **Missing Scale** `ACTIVE`

If he Charges and wins the Fight, all opponents are Knocked to Ground (except War Mumaks of Harad, Iron Hills Chariot, or any model that cannot be knocked Prone). If Smaug uses the Barge Brutal Power Attack, all models that Back Away suffer a Strength 9 hit.
