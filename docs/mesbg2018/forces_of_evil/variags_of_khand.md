## ARMY COMPOSITION

This army may include: Khandish King; Khandish Chieftain; Khandish Charioteer; Khandish Horseman; Khandish Warrior.

## ARMY BONUS

**"The Khandish Hosts"** - Khandish Horsemen and Khandish Charioteers do not count towards the force's Bow Limit.
