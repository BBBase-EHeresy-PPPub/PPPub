## ARMY COMPOSITION

This army may include: Suladan; Haradrim King; The Betrayer; The Golden King; Haradrim Chieftain; Haradrim Taskmaster; Hasharin; Raza, Fang of the Serpent; War Mumak of Harad; Abrakhan Merchant Guard; Haradrim Raider; Haradrim Warrior; Serpent Guard; Serpent Rider; Watcher of Karna.

## ARMY BONUS

**"The Scorpion's Sting"** - A serpent Horde army may have 50% of its warriors armed with bows. Additionally, all Haradrim Warriors and Raiders may apply the [Poisoned Weapons] special rule to all of their weapons.
