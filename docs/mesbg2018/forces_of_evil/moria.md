## ARMY COMPOSITION

This army may include: The Balrog; Dragon; Durburz; Ashrak; Druzhag; Groblog; Moria Blackshield Captain; Moria Goblin Captain; Cave Drake; Moria Blackshield Shaman; Moria Goblin Shaman; The Watcher in the Water; Bat Swarm; Cave Troll; Dweller in the Dark; Giant Spider; Moria Blackshield; Moria Blackshield Drummers; Moria Goblin Drum; Moria Goblin Prowler; Moria Goblin Warrior; Warg Marauder; Wild Warg.

## ARMY BONUS

**"We cannot get out, they are coming"** - Friendly Moria Goblin models involved in a Fight where their opponent is Trapped receive a bonus of +1 to their Fight value.

Whilst within water, The Watcher can choose a single Man-sized (or smaller) model and instantly slay them unless saved with Fate. The Watcher is removed from the board and needs to re-arrive as per the From the Deep special rule.
