## ARMY COMPOSITION

This army may include: Radagast the Brown; Beorn; Gwaihir; Great Eagle.

## ARMY BONUS

**"The Eagles are coming!"** - Eagle models from this army list gain a bonus of +1 to their Strength on a turn in which they charged.
