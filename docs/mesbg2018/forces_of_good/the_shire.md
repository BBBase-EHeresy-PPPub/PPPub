## ARMY COMPOSITION

This army may include: Bandobras Took; Gandalf the Grey; Meriadoc, Captain of the Shire; Peregrin, Captain of the Shire; Baldo Tulpenny; Bilbo Baggins; Farmer Maggot; Farmer Tolman Cotton; Frodo of the Nine Fingers; Hamfast 'Gaffer' Gamgee; Holfoot Bracegirdle; Lotho Sackville-Baggins; Paladin Took; Robin Smallburrow; Samwise the Brave; Will Whitfoot; Dunedain; Folco Boffin; Fredegar Bolger; Lobelia Sackville-Baggins; Rosie Cotton; Hobbit Archer; Hobbit Militia; Hobbit Shirriff.

## ARMY BONUS

**"Fear! Fire! Foes!"** - Hobbit models from this army list gain the [Woodland Creature] Special Rule.
