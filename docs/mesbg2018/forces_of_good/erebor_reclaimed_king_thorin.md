## ARMY COMPOSITION

This army may include: Thorin Oakenshield, King Under the Mountain; Balin the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Dain Ironfoot, Lord of the Iron Hills; Dwalin the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Iron Hills Chariot (Champions of Erebor); Bifur the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Bofur the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Bombur the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Dori the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Fili the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Gloin the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Iron Hills Captain; Kili the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Nori the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Oin the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Ori the Dwarf, Champion of Erebor; Iron Hills Dwarf; Iron Hills Goat Rider.

## ARMY BONUS

**"Will you follow me, one last time?"** - All friendly Erebor Dwarf models from this army list gain the Du Bekar special rule.

**"Du Bekar"** - A model with this special rule counts as being in range of a [banner][Banner] if they are within 6" of Thorin Oakenshield, King Under the Mountain.
