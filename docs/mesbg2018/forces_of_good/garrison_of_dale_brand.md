## ARMY COMPOSITION

This army may include: Brand, King of Dale; Bard II, Prince of Dale; Captain of Dale; Knight of Dale; Warrior of Dale; <span style="color: darkmagenta">Windlance</span>.

## ARMY BONUS

**"Girion, lord of the city, rallied his bowmen to fire upon the beast"** - Captains of Dale and Warriors of Dale have their Shoot value improved to 3+.
