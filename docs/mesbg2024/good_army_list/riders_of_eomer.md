## ADDITIONAL RULES

- A Riders of Eomer Army must include Eomer, who is always the Army's General.
- Riders of Rohan do not count towards this Army's Bow Limit.

## SPECIAL RULES

**Look to the sun** - Once per game, at the start of any friendly Activation Phase, Gandalf can use this special rule so long as he is alive and on the battlefield. If he does, then until the end of the turn, enemy models Engaged in Combat with a friendly Cavalry model that made a Charge that turn, or Supporting a Combat against a friendly Cavalry model that made a Charge that turn, suffer a -1 penalty to their Duel Roll.

**"Ride for ruin and the world's ending"** - Friendly Rohan Cavalry models gain a bonus of +1 to their Strength on a turn in which they Charge.

**"To the King!"** - Eomer may declare a [Heroic Combat] each turn for free.
