- Sting: 5 points

**MINOR HEROES**

## ADDITIONAL RULES

A Road to Rivendell Army must include Frodo. When writing a Road to Rivendell Army List, a player can either have all models in a single Warband or as multiple smaller warbands as normal.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"We do not stop 'til nightfall"** - Aragorn is determined to lead the Hobbits to safety and will not slow their path towards Rivendell. Aragorn may declare a Heroic March each turn for free. Additionally, friendly models that begin their Activation within 6" of Aragorn gain the Woodland Creature special rule until the end of the turn.

"I'm the faster rider. I'll take him" - Upon finding Aragorn and the Hobbits in the wilderness, Arwen sets Frodo onto Asfaloth and rides for Rivendell. If your Army includes both Frodo and Arwen, then Frodo may begin the game as a Passenger upon Asfaloth. Whilst Frodo is mounted on Asfaloth, Arwen gains the Resistant to Magic special rule, increases her Attacks characteristic to 3, and can spend Frodo's Might, Will and Fate Points as if they were her own. Frodo cannot put on the One Ring whilst he is a Passenger.

**"If you want him, come and claim him"** - Arwen will protect Frodo at all costs, even in the face of severe danger. Whilst she is within 6" of Frodo (including if he is a Passenger on Asfaloth), Arwen gains the Fearless special rule and may re-roll any dice when making a Casting Roll.

**The Cover of Night** - In order to travel as secretly as possible, the Hobbits will often end up using the dark of night to conceal their tracks. At the start of each game using this Army List, you may choose for the Scenario to take place at night if you wish. If you do, then at the start of each Move Phase you may choose whether or not the Gatekeeper of Bree's A Light in the Dark special rule is in effect or not.
