## SPECIAL RULES

"The Elves of Mirkwood are not like their kin. They are

**less wise, and more dangerous"** - The Elves of Mirkwood fight with a ruthless fury, especially when their king is present. Friendly Mirkwood Warrior models within 3" of Thranduil gain a bonus of +1 To Wound when making Strikes.

**Stoic Command** - The Elves follow their leaders' commands without hesitation. Friendly Warrior models treat your General as a banner.
