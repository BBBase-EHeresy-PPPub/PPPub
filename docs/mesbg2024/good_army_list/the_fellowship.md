## ADDITIONAL RULES

The Fellowship is always deployed as a single Warband.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"You have my sword"** - The Fellowship have pledged their lives to protecting Frodo, and will fight to the bitter end to ensure the safety of the Ringbearer. So long as Frodo is alive and on the battlefield, friendly Fellowship models gain the Fearless special rule. Additionally, a Fellowship Army can never be considered Broken whilst Frodo is alive, even if he escapes the battlefield in Scenarios where this applies.

**The Bond of Fellowship** - When forced to fight, the members of the Fellowship find their strength in the presence of their friends and allies. Friendly models within 3" of another friendly Fellowship model may re-roll a single failed To Wound Roll when making Strikes.

**Traverse the Wilderness** - Following setting out from Rivendell, the Fellowship have had to navigate all manner of terrain, and have become adept at moving swiftly to avoid being seen. Friendly Fellowship models gain the Woodland Creature and Mountain Dweller special rules.
