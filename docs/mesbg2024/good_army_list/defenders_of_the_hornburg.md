## SPECIAL RULES

**Defend the Hornburg** - The Rohirrim within the Hornburg find themselves backed against a wall and will give their all to defend what little they have left. Friendly Rohan Infantry gain the Dominant (2) special rule.

**A Desperate Defence** - In dire situations such as these, the Rohirrim are forced to fight with desperation against their foes, though those fighting for their lives often find their inner strength. Friendly Rohan Infantry Warrior models must re-roll To Wound Rolls of a natural 1 when making Strikes.

**Frenzy of the King** - The sight of a crazed and wild-eyed Helm Hammerhand ripping his foes apart is a truly terrifying sight and enough to make those who stand against him turn tail and flee. At the start of the End Phase of a turn in which Helm Hammerhand kills an enemy Hero or Monster, enemy models within 3" of Helm must take a Courage Test in an order chosen by Helm's controlling player. If the test is failed, the model must Move up to its Move Value directly away from Helm.
