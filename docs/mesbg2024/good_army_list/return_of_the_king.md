## ADDITIONAL RULES

- A Return of the King Army must include Aragorn (Strider) and the King of the Dead. Aragorn is always the Army's General.

## SPECIAL RULES

**The Heir of Isildur** - Aragorn does not have to pay any points for Andúril, Flame of the West; he automatically has this upgrade

**"Fight for us and regain your honour"** - Friendly Dunharrow Warrior models within 3" of Aragorn gain a bonus of +1 to their Fight Value.

**Visions of Terror** - Whenever the King of the Dead Charges into Combat, after the Charge has been completed, he may spend a Will Point to use this special rule. If he does, enemy models within 3" of the King of the Dead must take a Courage Test in an order chosen by the King of the Dead's controlling player. If the test is failed, the model must Move up to its Move Value directly away from the King of the Dead.
