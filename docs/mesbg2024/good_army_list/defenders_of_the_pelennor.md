## ADDITIONAL RULES

- Rohan Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Rohan Hero.
- Gondor Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Gondor Hero or Gandalf the White.
- Dunharrow Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Dunharrow Hero, or in the Warband of Aragorn, Legolas or Gimli.

## SPECIAL RULES

**Defend the White City** - Each Hero in your Army can, once per game, declare a [Heroic Combat] for free.
