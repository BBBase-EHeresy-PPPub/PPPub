## ADDITIONAL RULES

When writing an Army List for The Shire, if the Army contains only Hero models a player can either have models in a single Warband or as multiple smaller warbands as normal.

## SPECIAL RULES

**Bilbo's Magic Ring** - Before the Hobbits set out on the

**journey, it is Bilbo who carries the One Ring** - not that he knows the power of what he carries. In this Army List, Bilbo carries the One Ring instead of Frodo.

**"This'll be a night to remember"** - The Hobbits of the Shire enjoy nothing more than a party to leave them in high spirits. Friendly models may re-roll failed Courage Tests. Additionally, friendly models cannot have their Courage Tests reduced by the special rules or Magical Powers of enemy models.

**The Grey Wizard** - Though some see Gandalf as a disturber of the peace, many Hobbits respect and admire the old

**Wizard** - especially his fireworks. Friendly models may benefit from Gandalf's Stand Fast. Additionally, Gandalf may use two fireworks each Shoot Phase rather than one, though they must be different fireworks and must target different models.

A Long-expected Party - Filled with merriment and ale, the Hobbits' spirits are as high as they can be and many feel they can take on anything life would throw at them. Friendly models will pass a Fate Roll on a 3+ rather than a 4+. Additionally, friendly models may re-roll failed Fate Rolls.
