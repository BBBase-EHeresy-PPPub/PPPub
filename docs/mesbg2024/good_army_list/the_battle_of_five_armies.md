## ADDITIONAL RULES

Elf Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of an Elf Hero. Dwarf Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Dwarf Hero. Lake-town Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Lake-town Hero, or in the Warband of Gandalf the Grey or Bilbo Baggins. Eagle Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of an Eagle Hero, or in the Warband of Radagast the Brown or Beorn. The Battle of Five Armies may not include more Fledgeling Great Eagles than Great Eagles.

## SPECIAL RULES

**We stand together** - Man, Elf, Dwarf and beast all fight together as one in the face of the Evil amassed against them. Friendly models may re-roll To Wound Rolls of a natural 1 when making Strikes if they are involved in a Combat with a friendly model that has a different racial Keyword to themselves.
