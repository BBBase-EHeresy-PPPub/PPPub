## SPECIAL RULES

**Great Resilience** - Whenever a friendly Bear model suffers a Wound, roll a D6.On the roll of a natural 6, the Wound is ignored.

**Natural Resistance** - Friendly models gain the [Resistant to Magic] special rule.

**Ursine Fury** - At the beginning of each Move Phase, before the Declare Heroic Action step, you may roll a D6 for each friendly Bear model you have on the battlefield. If any of the D6 roll a natural 6, then for the duration of the turn enemy models suffer a -1 penalty to any Courage Tests they are required to take as a result of trying to Charge a model with the [Terror] special rule.
