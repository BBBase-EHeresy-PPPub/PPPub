# Fields of Celebrant [Legacy]

Army must always contain a King of Men, which must choose the Gondor keyword as part of its Kingdoms of Men special rule. No other Kings of Men can be taken. « Rohan Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Rohan Hero. « Gondor Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Gondor Hero.

## SPECIAL RULES

**Birth of an Alliance** - Friendly models within 6" of both a friendly Rohan Hero model and a friendly Gondor Hero model gain a bonus of +1 to any Courage or Intelligence Tests they are required to take.

**Gondor's Last Defence** - Friendly Gondor models within range of an Objective Marker may re-roll To Wound rolls of a natural 1 when making Strikes.

**TTe Charge of Eorl** - Once per game, at the start of any friendly Activation Phase, Eorl can use this special rule so long as he is alive and on the battleiield, and has the Cavalry keyword. If he does, then until the end of the turn all friendly Cavalry models must Charge when they Move if able. Additionally, until the end of the turn, friendly Cavalry models that Charged this turn may re-roll any failed To Wound rolls when making Strikes.
