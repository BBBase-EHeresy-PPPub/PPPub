## SPECIAL RULES

**"They say a great sorceress lives in these woods..."** - Friendly Lothlorien models gain the [Resistant to Magic] special rule.

**"The Dwarf breathes so loud, we could have shot him in the dark"** - Friendly Lothlorien models may re-roll To Hit rolls of a 1 when making a shooting attack if the target of the shooting attack is unable to see them due to the effects of an [Elven cloak][Elven Cloak].
