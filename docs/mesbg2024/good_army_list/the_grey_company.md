## ADDITIONAL RULES

- A Grey Company Army must always contain Aragorn, who is always the Army's General.
- A Grey Company Army may be deployed as a single Warband, with Aragorn as the Warband's Captain, regardless of the number of models.

## SPECIAL RULES

**The Heir of Isildur** - Aragorn does not have to pay any points for Andúril, Flame of the West; he automatically has this upgrade - it is free.

**"They're dangerous folk, wandering the wilds"** - Rangers of the North increase their Attacks value to 2.

**Passing of the Grey Company** - Rangers of the North may benefit from the [Stand Fast] of any friendly Unique Hero model.

**Masters of the Wild** - Friendly models are not slowed by difficult terrain of any type.
