## ADDITIONAL RULES

A Breaking of the Fellowship Army must include Aragorn (Strider), who is always the Army's General. All Fellowship models must be included in Aragorn's Warband. Lothlórien Warriors can only be included in a Warband led by Haldir.

## SPECIAL RULES

**Gifts of Galadriel** - Upon leaving Lothlórien, the Fellowship received a number of gifts from Galadriel to aid them in their journey ahead. Each Fellowship model will gain the Wargear listed next to their name below, which have the effects detailed:

**Frodo** - Light of Eärendil ACTIVE - At the start of each of his Activations, Frodo may roll a D6. On a 3+, Frodo counts as having Cast the Blinding Light Magical Power. Sam - Elven Rope ACTIVE - Sam always counts as rolling a 6 for any Climb, Leap or Jump Tests he is required to make. Merry & Pippin - Daggers of the Noldorin ACTIVE - These are Elven hand weapons. Whilst wielding these weapons, Merry and Pippin may re-roll failed To Wound Rolls when making Strikes.

**Aragorn** - Elven Dagger ACTIVE - This is an Elven hand weapon with the Uruk-haibane special rule. Legolas - Bow of the Galadhrim ACTIVE - Whilst within 3" of a friendly Lothlórien model, Legolas may re-roll failed To Hit Rolls when making shooting attacks. Gimli - Galadriel's Locks ACTIVE - Gimli may use this Wargear to re-roll a single D6 in a Duel Roll. Gimli may only use this Wargear three times during the course of the game.

**Boromir** - Golden Belt ACTIVE - Boromir gains the Woodland Creature special rule.

**"You have my sword"** - The Fellowship have pledged their lives to protecting Frodo and will fight to the bitter end to ensure the safety of the Ringbearer. So long as Frodo is alive and on the battlefield, friendly Fellowship models gain the Fearless special rule. Additionally, a Breaking of the Fellowship Army can never be considered Broken whilst Frodo is alive, even if he escapes the battlefield in Scenarios where this applies.

**Blessing of Galadriel** - Whilst within the borders of the Golden Wood, the Fellowship remain under the protection of the Lady Galadriel. Friendly models count as being under the effects of the Fortify Spirit Magical Power, even if they have no Will Points remaining.

**Shielded from Unfriendly Eyes** - The Elves of Lothlórien use cloaks to remain unseen and have gifted such clothing to the Fellowship so they can evade the spies of the enemy. Friendly Hero models gain an Elven cloak for free.
