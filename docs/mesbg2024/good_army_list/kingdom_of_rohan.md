## SPECIAL RULES

**"Ride for ruin and the world's ending"** - Such is the ferocity in which the Rohirrim charge, that those who attempt to stand against them will be flattened where they stand. Friendly Rohan Cavalry models gain a bonus of +1 to their Strength on a turn in which they Charge.

**Home of the Horse Lords** - The Rohirrim are the greatest horsemen in Middle-earth, and they have a real bond with the horses they ride. When rolling for their Horse Lord special rule, friendly models will pass the roll on a natural 5+ rather than a natural 6.
