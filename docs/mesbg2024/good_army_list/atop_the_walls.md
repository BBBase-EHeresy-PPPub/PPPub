## ADDITIONAL RULES

An Atop the Walls Army must include Gandalf the White, who is always the Army's General.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"Fight to the last Man"** - The presence of the White Wizard is just what the Men of Gondor need to ensure they stand their ground and fight. Friendly Gondor models within 12" of Gandalf the White automatically pass any Courage Tests they have to take.

**"Return to your posts"** - Gandalf's orders to those in the White City are to stand and fight, and protect the posts they have been assigned to. Friendly Gondor models within range of an Objective Marker may re-roll To Wound Rolls of a natural 1 when making shooting attacks or making Strikes.

**"Send these foul creatures into the abyss"** - Under the command of Gandalf, the defenders of Minas Tirith are determined to push back the threat of Mordor. Friendly Gondor models within 12" of Gandalf the White gain the Hatred (Mordor) special rule.
