## ADDITIONAL RULES

- A Men of the West Army must include Aragorn, King Elessar, who is always the Army's General.
- If your Army includes any Cavalry models then it cannot include any Eagle models, and vice versa.
- Only Gwaihir can include Great Eagles in his Warband, and Gwaihir can only include Great Eagles in his Warband.
- Gandalf the White does not have his [Staff of Power] in this Army.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"For Frodo!"** - Once per game, at the start of any Fight Phase, Aragorn can use this special rule so long as he is alive and on the battlefield. If he does, then friendly non-Eagle models within 6" of Aragorn gain a bonus of +1 Fight Value and may re-roll To Wound rolls of a natural 1 when making Strikes until the end of the turn. This is treated as an Active ability belonging to Aragorn.

**"This day we fight!"** - So long as Aragorn is alive and on the battlefield, friendly models automatically pass any Courage Tests they are required to take.

**The Final Stand** - Friendly Infantry models within 3" of a friendly Hero model gain the [Dominant] (2) special rule.

**The White Wizard** - Gandalf may re-roll a single D6 in any Casting Rolls he makes.
