## ADDITIONAL RULES

- An Army of Carn Dum must always contain Aldrac, who is always the Army's General.

## SPECIAL RULES

**Show of Strength** - If a Carn Dum Hero slays one or more enemy models in Combat, then for the duration of the following turn they gain a bonus of +1 to their Fight Value.

**Banners of Carn Dum** - Friendly banners have a range of 6" rather than 3".

**Fierce Charge** - Friendly Warrior models gain a bonus of +1 to their Fight Value in a turn in which they Charge.
