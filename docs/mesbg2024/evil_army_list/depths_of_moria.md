## SPECIAL RULES

**Drums in the Deep** - The sound of drums can be heard echoing throughout the caverns of Moria, heralding impending doom for the enemies of Moria and driving the Goblins forth into battle. Enemy models suffer a -1 penalty to any Courage Tests they are required to take, whilst friendly Moria Goblin models gain a bonus of +1 to any Courage Tests they are required to take. Additionally, friendly Moria Goblin models always count as being in range of a banner.

**Dominion of the Balrog** - When the Balrog stands with them, the Goblins of Moria feel almost invincible and will fight with redoubled fury. Friendly Moria Goblin models within 6" of the Balrog gain a bonus of +1 to their Fight Value. Additionally, so long as the Balrog has at least 6 Wounds remaining, a Depths of Moria Army can never count as being Broken. Should the Balrog have 5 or fewer Wounds remaining, a Depths of Moria Army will Break as normal. However, should the Balrog ever be removed as a casualty, a Depths of Moria Army will immediately count as being Broken.

**Shadow…** - The Balrog is cloaked in darkness, making it almost impossible to see where the shadows end and the Balrog begins. Shooting attacks that target the Balrog will only ever hit on the roll of a 5+.

**…and Flame** - By wreathing itself in blisteringly hot flames, the Balrog is capable of unleashing its fires upon those who stand before it. Once per game, at the start of any Shoot Phase, the Balrog may select a single enemy model within 3" of it and roll a D6. On a 2+, the model is immediately Set Ablaze. This is an Active ability belonging to the Balrog, and after it has used this ability, the Balrog no longer benefits from the 'Shadow…' special rule.
