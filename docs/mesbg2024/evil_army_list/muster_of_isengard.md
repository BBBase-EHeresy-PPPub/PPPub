**HEROES OF FORTITUDE**

- Uruk-hai Captain: 65 points

## ADDITIONAL RULES

A Muster of Isengard Army must always contain Saruman, who is always the Army's General.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"You do not know pain, you do not know fear"** - Saruman has drilled into his Uruk-hai that they are all-but unkillable, something they all seemingly believe. The Break Point for a Muster of Isengard Army is 66% rather than 50%.

**Isengard Unleashed** - As the armies of Orthanc empty from Isengard, they march relentlessly towards Helm's Deep to bring death to Rohan. During the first turn of the game, each friendly Uruk-hai Hero may declare a Heroic March for free. Additionally, Uruk-hai Captains gain Heroic Strike.

**"There will be no dawn for Men"** - Saruman has drilled his fighting Uruk-hai to show no mercy, especially against the race of Men. Friendly Uruk-hai models gain the Hatred (Man) special rule.
