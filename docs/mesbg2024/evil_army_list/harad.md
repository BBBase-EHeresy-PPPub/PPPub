## ADDITIONAL RULES

- A Harad Army may have up to 50% of its Warrior models armed with bows rather than the usual 33%.

## SPECIAL RULES

**War Beasts from the South** - Friendly Mûmak models gain the [Harbinger of Evil] (12") special rule.

**The Scorpion's Sting** - Friendly models with the [Poisoned Attacks] (bow) special rule exchange it for [Poisoned Attacks] on all of their weapons.

**Monstrous Rampage** - Each Hero that is commanding a Mûmak can, once per game, declare a [Heroic Combat] for free whilst atop a Mûmak. When they do, the Mûmak will be affected as if they had been the one to declare the [Heroic Combat], though they may not pivot before they Trample as part of a [Heroic Combat].
