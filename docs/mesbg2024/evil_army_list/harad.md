# HARAD

**HEROES OF LEGEND**

- Mûmak War Leader: 400 points

**HEROES OF FORTITUDE**

- Haradrim Chieftain: 50 points
- Bow: 5 points
- War Mûmak of Harad: 250 points
- Gnarled Hide: 25 points
- Mahûd Beastmaster Chieftain: 25 points
- Rocks: 20 points
- Sigils of Defiance: 20 points
- Tusk Weapons: 20 points
- Foul Temperament: 15 points

**WARRIORS**

- Haradrim Warrior: 6 points
- Banner: 25 points
- War horn: 25 points
- Bow: 1 point
- Spear: 1 point

## ADDITIONAL RULES

A Harad Army may have up to 50% of its Warrior models armed with bows rather than the usual 33%.

## SPECIAL RULES

**War Beasts from the South** - The sight of the great, lumbering beasts rampaging towards an army is enough to instil terror in even the bravest warriors. Friendly Mûmak models gain the Harbinger of Evil (12") special rule.

**The Scorpion's Sting** - The Haradrim are known to coat their weapons in exotic toxins, turning what would be a mere flesh wound into a fatal injury. Friendly models with the Poisoned Attacks (bow) special rule exchange it for Poisoned Attacks on all of their weapons.

**Monstrous Rampage** - Bringing a charging Mûmak to a complete stop is an all but impossible task save for actually slaying the beast. Each Hero that is commanding a Mûmak can, once per game, declare a Heroic Combat for free whilst atop a Mûmak. When they do, the Mûmak will be affected as if they had been the one to declare the Heroic Combat, though they may not pivot before they Trample as part of a Heroic Combat.
