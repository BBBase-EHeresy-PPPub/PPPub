## ADDITIONAL RULES

- A Barad-dur Army must always contain Sauron, who is always the Army's General.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"The power of the Ring could not be undone"** - So long as Sauron has at least 3 Wounds remaining a Barad-dUr Army can never count as being Broken. Should Sauron have 2 or fewer Wounds remaining, a Barad-dûr Army will Break as normal. However, should Sauron ever be removed as a casualty, a Barad-dur Army will immediately count as being Broken.

**Teeming with Orcs** - Sauron's Warband may include 24 models rather than the usual 18 for a Hero of Legend.

**Victory is at hand** - So long as Sauron is alive and on the battlefield friendly Orc models gain a +1 bonus to any Courage Tests they are required to take.
