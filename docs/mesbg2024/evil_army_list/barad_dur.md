## ADDITIONAL RULES

A Barad-dûr Army must always contain Sauron, who is always the Army's General.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"The power of the Ring could not be undone"** - Sauron is the lynchpin of his armies and whilst he still lives, his Orcs will fight on. So long as Sauron has at least 3 Wounds remaining, a Barad- dûr Army can never count as being Broken. Should Sauron have 2 or fewer Wounds remaining, a Barad-dûr Army will Break as normal. However, should Sauron ever be removed as a casualty, a Barad-dûr Army will immediately count as being Broken.

**Teeming with Orcs** - At the height of the Dark Lord's power, the lands of Mordor are filled with bands of Orcs, all of which will fight in Sauron's armies. Sauron's Warband may include 24 models rather than the usual 18 for a Hero of Legend.

**Victory is at hand** - With their master striding into battle alongside them, the Orcs of Mordor feel as if there is no way they can be defeated. So long as Sauron is alive and on the battlefield, friendly Orc models gain a +1 bonus to any Courage Tests they are required to take.
