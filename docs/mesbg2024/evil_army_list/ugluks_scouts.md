# UGLUK'S SCOUTS

## ARMY COMPOSITION

**HEROES OF VALOUR**

- Uglúk, Uruk-hai Scout Captain: 75 points

**HEROES OF FORTITUDE**

- Grishnákh, Orc Captain: 55 points
- Snaga, Orc Captain: 50 points
- Uruk-hai Scout Captain: 55 points
- Isengard Orc Captain: 45 points

**INDEPENDENT HEROES**

- Uruk-hai Drummer: 35 points

**WARRIORS**

- Uruk-hai Scout: 8 points
- Banner: 25 points
- Shield: 1 point
- Uruk-hai bow: 1 point
- Isengard Orc Warrior: 5 points
- Banner: 25 points
- Orc bow: 1 point
- Shield: 1 point
- Spear: 1 point

## ADDITIONAL RULES

An Uglúk's Scouts Army must always contain Uglúk, who is always the Army's General. Orc Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of an Orc Hero. Uruk-hai Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of an Uruk-hai Hero.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"Their pace has quickened"** - Under the orders of Saruman, the Uruk-hai will march ceaselessly to find the Halflings and bring them to him, no matter what stands in their path. Friendly Uruk-hai models increase their Move Value to 8". Additionally, friendly Uruk-hai models gain the Woodland Creature special rule.

**"Get a fire going"** - When required to rest, the Uruk-hai will make camp and secure the area while they take a breather. Once per game, in Scenarios that use Objective Markers, Uglúk can use this special rule during his Move to choose an Objective Marker in base contact. So long as Uglúk is alive and on the battlefield, friendly models within range of the chosen Objective Marker gain the Dominant (2) special rule.

**Animosity** - The Orcs and Uruk-hai harbour a great disdain for one another and will take any chance to prove they are better than the other. Friendly Orc models gain a bonus of +1 To Wound when making Strikes if there is a friendly Uruk-hai model Engaged in the same Combat. Friendly Uruk-hai models gain a bonus of +1 To Wound when making Strikes if there is a friendly Orc model Engaged in the same Combat.
