# ASSAULT UPON HELM'S DEEP

## ARMY COMPOSITION

**HEROES OF FORTITUDE**

- Uruk-hai Captain: 65 points

**WARRIORS**

- Uruk-hai Warrior: 9 points
- Banner: 25 points
- Crossbow: 2 points
- Pike: 1 point
- Shield: 1 point
- Uruk-hai Berserker: 15 points
- Uruk-hai Demolition Team: 80 points
- Isengard Assault Ballista: 75 points

## ADDITIONAL RULES

An Assault Upon Helm's Deep Army must always contain an Uruk-hai Captain, who is always the Army's General.

## SPECIAL RULES

**"You do not know pain, you do not know fear"** - Saruman has drilled into his Uruk-hai that they are all-but unkillable, something they all seemingly believe. The Break Point for an Assault Upon Helm's Deep Army is 66% rather than 50%.

Commander of the Uruk-hai - The commanders of Saruman's forces are the strongest and most vicious Uruk-hai to come out of the pits beneath Isengard. The Uruk-hai Captain that is your General increases its Attacks and Wounds to 3 each.

**Break the Walls** - Saruman has unleashed his most devastating weapons of war upon Helm's Deep, attempting to bring down the walls with a mixture of metal and fire. Friendly Isengard Assault Ballistae may re-roll To Hit and Scatter Rolls of a 1. Additionally, when a friendly Demolition Charge is detonated, you may roll two dice on the Detonation Table rather than one and pick either result.
