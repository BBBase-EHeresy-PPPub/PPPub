## ADDITIONAL RULES

If your Army includes Freca, then he must be your General. Rohirrim Traitors can only be included in Lord Thorne's Warband.

## SPECIAL RULES

**Whipped into a frenzy** - The Hill Tribesmen have been driven into a frenzy by the promise of claiming the lands they believe to be theirs. Whenever a Hill Tribesman or Hill Tribes Chieftain suffers a Wound, roll a D6. On a natural 6, the Wound is ignored.

**Barbarous Killers** - Freca and his son are determined to claim the throne for themselves, and will slay any who stand in their way without mercy. Friendly Dunland models gain a bonus of +1 To Wound when making Strikes against enemy Hero models.
