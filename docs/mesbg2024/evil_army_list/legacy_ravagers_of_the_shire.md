## ADDITIONAL RULES

- A Ravagers of the Shire Army must always contain Golfimbul, who is always the Army's General.

## SPECIAL RULES

**Golfimbul's Rage** - Golfimbul may declare a [Heroic Combat] each turn for free.

**Courage from Ignorance** - Whilst Golfimbul is still alive and on the battlefield, friendly Orc models gain a bonus of +1 to any Courage Tests they are required to take.

**Overwhelm the Foe** - Friendly Orc models gain a bonus of +1 To Wound when making Strikes against an enemy model that is outnumbered in a Combat.
