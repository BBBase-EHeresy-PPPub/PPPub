**SPECIAL RULES**

* **Broken Mind** `ACTIVE` - The loss of Boromir has driven Denethor to
* madness, and there are times when he cannot recognise friend from foe.
* During each Priority Phase, after Priority has been determined,
* Denethor must take an Intelligence Test. If the test is passed, all is fine
* and Denethor acts as normal. If the test is failed, then during this turn
* Denethor is controlled by the opposing player and will be Activated

* him if they beat him in a Combat. When Denethor is under the control of

* always counts as a model in his controlling player's Army.
* If Boromir is part of the same Army, then Denethor will automatically
* pass this Intelligence Test so long as Boromir is alive. However, should
* Boromir be slain, then Denethor will automatically fail the next
* Intelligence Test he has to make for this special rule.
