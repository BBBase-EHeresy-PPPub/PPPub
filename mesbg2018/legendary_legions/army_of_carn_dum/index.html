    Army of Carn Dum

    ARMY COMPOSITION

    • ALDRAC: 120 points
    • ANGMAR ORC CAPTAIN: 40 points
      • Warg: 10 points
      • Orc bow: 5 points
      • Shield: 5 points
    • CAPTAIN OF CARN DUM: 55 points
    • FRAECHT: 65 points
    • ANGMAR ORC WARRIOR: 5 points
      • Banner: 25 points
      • Orc bow: 1 point
      • Shield: 1 point
      • Spear: 1 point
      • Two-Handed Weapon: 1 point
    • ANGMAR WARG RIDER: 11 points
      • Banner: 25 points
      • Orc bow: 1 point
      • Shield: 1 point
      • Throwing Spears: 1 point
    • ORC TRACKER: 5 points
      • Warg: 7 points
    • WARRIOR OF CARN DUM: 9 points
      • Banner: 25 points
      • Spear: 1 point

    ADDITIONAL RULES

    • Your force must always include the Aldrac, who must always be the leader.

    SPECIAL RULES

    "Show of Strength" - If a Carn-Dum Hero kills one or more enemy models in combat, then for the duration of the following turn they gain a bonus of +1 to their Fight value.

    "Banners of Carn Dum" - The range of banners in this force is increased to 6".

    "Words of Power" - The range of all of Fraecht's Incantations of Power are increased to 6".
